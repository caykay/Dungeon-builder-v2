Detailed Description

The Doorway class.

This function specifies the content that may be present on a roomedge. It inherits RoomEdge class. It specifies properties of a doorway and defines interactions with other doorway specifically when one doorway connects with another doorway of an opposite room. Or if a doorway is an exit or entrance and more.
