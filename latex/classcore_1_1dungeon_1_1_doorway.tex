\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classcore_1_1dungeon_1_1_doorway}{Doorway}} class. 

This function specifies the content that may be present on a roomedge. It inherits \mbox{\hyperlink{classcore_1_1dungeon_1_1_room_edge}{Room\+Edge}} class. It specifies properties of a doorway and defines interactions with other doorway specifically when one doorway connects with another doorway of an opposite room. Or if a doorway is an exit or entrance and more.
